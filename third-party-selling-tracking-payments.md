# Third Party Selling and Tracking Payments

- Overview: How ZIP Handles 3rd Party Reselling

ZIP treats 3rd party resellers the same way an affiliate network or agent portal does. The reseller gets a portion of the ticket price (their commission), and ZIP automatically calculates and tracks what you earn.

- Model	What it means	How ZIP handles it
Standard Sale	Customer buys directly from you	ZIP processes payment, pays you

Reseller Sale	3rd party (affiliate/agent) sells your tickets	ZIP attributes sale, splits payment, tracks both parties

Marketplace Sale	Customer buys from secondary market (StubHub, etc.)	ZIP ingests data via APIs, tracks your cut
